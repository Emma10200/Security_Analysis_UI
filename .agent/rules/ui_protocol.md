1. **READ ONLY**: You may read `data/*.parquet`. You may NEVER modify them.
2. **POLARS ONLY**: Use Polars for data manipulation. No Pandas.
3. **VIBE**: High density, dark mode, institutional grade. No whitespace.
4. **INTENTIONALITY (CRITICAL)**:
   - Do NOT add "placeholder" features.
   - Do NOT add random charts "just to see data."
